Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about DSOG, our programs, and how we can help your business succeed.

What is DSOG?

DSOG is an entrepreneurship support organization dedicated to empowering businesses, startups, and young entrepreneurs through comprehensive training, events, and practical startup opportunities.

What programs do you offer?

We're developing entrepreneurship training programs, business bootcamps, skill development workshops, and startup support services. Our programs focus on practical, hands-on learning experiences.

How can I participate in DSOG programs?

You can join our programs by contacting us through our website, registering for upcoming events on our bootcamp page, or reaching out directly for training opportunities.

Do you provide mentorship?

Yes, we're building a mentorship program that connects aspiring entrepreneurs with experienced business professionals for guidance and support throughout their entrepreneurial journey.
