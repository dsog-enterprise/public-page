Our Founding Vision

DSOG was founded in 2020 with a clear vision: to create a platform where businesses, startups, and young entrepreneurs could find the comprehensive support they need to thrive in today's competitive landscape.

We recognized that many aspiring entrepreneurs had brilliant ideas but lacked the guidance, training, and practical opportunities to turn those ideas into successful enterprises.

Problem Identified

Recognized the gap between entrepreneurial ambition and practical business success

Solution Created

Developed a comprehensive ecosystem of training, events, and support systems

Impact Delivered

Transformed hundreds of business ideas into successful, sustainable enterprises

Our Milestones

2020: Foundation

DSOG was founded with a mission to empower entrepreneurs through practical business support and training programs.

2021: First Events

Launched our first entrepreneurship events, engaging 100+ entrepreneurs in hands-on business workshops and networking sessions.

2022: Ecosystem Growth

Expanded our services to include franchise opportunities, startup incubation, and comprehensive training programs.

2023: Impact Scaling

2024: Future Vision

Expanding our reach to empower 1000+ entrepreneurs annually and launching new enterprise development initiatives.

The DSOG Approach

We engage, train, and empower entrepreneurs through a proven three-phase approach.

Engage Through Events

We organize impactful events that engage entrepreneurs in hands-on experiences, networking opportunities, and practical business challenges that shape their business acuity.

  • Business pitch competitions
  • Industry networking mixers
  • Expert workshop series

Train Professionals

Comprehensive training programs designed to equip professionals and entrepreneurs with the skills needed to thrive in today's competitive business landscape.

  • Business fundamentals & strategy
  • Digital marketing & e-commerce
  • Financial management & funding
